1. Day Out Pass
A Day Out Pass allows a student to leave the university campus for a short duration within the
same day. To apply for a Day Out Pass, students are required to apply through the official CamU
mobile application. Parent approval through the CamU app is mandatory for the Day Out Pass.
Students availing a Day Out Pass must return to the university campus by 10:00 PM on the same
day. No warden approval is required for a Day Out Pass provided all conditions are fulfilled.
2. Leave Out Pass
A Leave Out Pass is required when a student intends to leave the campus for more than one day or
overnight. To apply for a Leave Out Pass, students must first apply through the CamU mobile
application. Parent approval through the CamU app is mandatory. In addition to parent approval,
warden approval is compulsory for a Leave Out Pass. Students must send an official email
request to their respective hostel warden for approval. The Leave Out Pass is considered valid only
after confirmation from both the parent and the warden.
Note:
Failure to follow the above outpass procedures may result in disciplinary action as per university
rules.
